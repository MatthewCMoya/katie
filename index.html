Katie Moya

Professional Entertainer Vocal Specialist.

Bio

Katie Moya is a Los Angeles-based musical theatre performer with a strong background in vocal performance and comedic acting. Having graduated from AMDA College and Conservatory of the Performing Arts with a Certificate in Musical Theatre, she understands the need to soak up as much knowledge and experience as she can in a short period of time. Her love for theatre began at a very young age, and compelled her to pursue every training opportunity she could. Katie was heavily involved with theatre throughout high school and regularly trained in improv, acting, and classical vocal technique. Her passion for performing eventually led her to AMDA, where she thrived working under Broadway professionals daily through-out the two year program. Shortly before graduation, Katie was offered the pleasure to work as a principal vocalist in Hong Kong Disneyland. Not only did she take it, but she stayed in Hong Kong for nearly two years, performing and traveling around most of Southeast Asia. Her experiences in Asia have shaped her into a curious, hungry, and well-rounded performer. She is now working in regional theatre along the West Coast and is looking forward to more travels and adventures. In her spare time, Katie loves to cook and read. She's also an accomplished manicurist, make up artist and Randy Newman impressionist.

Project Details

My Performance Experiences

I've been extremely blessed to be able to perform in amazing theaters with fantastic performers up and down the West Coast and across the Pacific Ocean in Asia. The opportunity to perform at Hong Kong Disneyland came only weeks after graduating from AMDA College and Conservatory of the Performing Arts, and it taught me more about performing than I expected. I spent 22 months singing in The Golden Mickeys as Belle and I was lucky enough to be in the very last performance of the show, concluding a 10 year run. While at Hong Kong Disneyland I also had the opportunity to play Miss Kitty--a saucy saloon proprietor in The Grizzly Gulch Welcome Wagon Show. A renowned hilarious atmosphere hootenanny in the middle of the Park. After leaving Hong Kong, I immediately began working on Cinderella, which has been such a rewarding experience. We had the chance to retell this amazing story with the most fantastic music in front of an adorable and eager audience. I'm looking forward to the start of my career in Los Angeles this year and I hope to bring my education and experiences with me into every "job" I have the pleasure of doing.
